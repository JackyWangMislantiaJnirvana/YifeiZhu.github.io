Research

The Morava E-homology of Bousfield-Kuhn functors on odd-dimensional spheres, in progress

We give explicit presentations for the completed E-homology of the Bousfield-Kuhn functor on odd-dimensional spheres at chromatic level 2, and compare them to the level 1 case.

Deformation structures and norm coherence, in preparation

We generalize Ando's construction of H∞ complex orientations on Morava E-theories associated to Honda formal group laws over Fp. In particular, we consider formal group laws defined over algebraic extensions of Fp.
